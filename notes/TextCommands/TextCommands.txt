Working with "||":
	p{predef number}; - Creates party color effect
	c{predef number}; - Color function, paints you words in certain color.
	w{predef number}; - Creates predef wave effect, basically x-y modifier. Work good on any length  
	wl{predef number}; - Works as "w", but working good on a long distance commands
	d{any number} - creates delay between charasters. -1, removes delay from section
Working with "<>":
	*<p:{predef name};> - replase it with specified string 
	<!global:{ }:{ };> - affects global value, outside his textbox
	
	<!local:{ }:{ };> - affects local value, inside his text
		<!local:delay:off/on;>
